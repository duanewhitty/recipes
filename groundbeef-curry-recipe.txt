Lean ground beef - 1kg
Minced garlic - 1 clove
Minced ginger - equal to half-clove garlic
2 large onions
White mushrooms - 250g
1 Large Red bell pepper - chopped
1 Large Yellow bell pepper - chopped
30 ml Madras prepared curry paste
3 cups cooked whole long-grain brown rice
Brown garlic and ginger in butter
Add ground beef and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes
Add mushrooms and onions and cook with ground beef for 5 minutes
Add peppers to pan
Stir curry into 2 cups of water and add to pan
Bring to boil
Cook for 20 minutes on low heat
